Konno, Makoto

Makoto is a regular high school student who spends her time playing baseball with her two best friends, Chiaki and Kousuke. She always sleeps in a few minutes too many, is remarkably clumsy, hates pop quizzes like every other high school student and never really makes an effort to brush her wayward hair.

Mamiya, Chiaki

One of Makoto's two best friends. Chiaki is easygoing and laidback. He transferred to Makoto's school in the spring and quickly became fast friends with Makoto and Kousuke, forming a steadfast trio between the three.

Tsuda, Kousuke

One of Makoto's two best friends. Kousuke is smart and level-headed. He is the voice of reason between the three friends. Kousuke wants to go into a medical profession and constantly thinks about studying, but finds time to play baseball and hang out with Makoto and Chiaki.

Yoshiyama, Kazuko

Makoto's aunt.

Takase, Soujirou

Makoto's classmate who appears during their cooking class. He is subject to bullying and later on gets into conflict with Makoto.

Konno, Miyuki

Makoto's sister.

Fujitani, Kaho

One of Makoto's classmates at school. She's got a huge crush on Makoto's friend, Kousuke. Later in the story, Makoto helps her get together with Kousuke.

Hayakawa, Yuri

Makoto's best friend.

Fukushima-sensei

Their subject teacher in maths.
